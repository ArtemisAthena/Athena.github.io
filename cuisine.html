PECKISH? CHECK OUT THESE PLACES!

Đồng Văn Market

Located in Dong Van old town, this place is bustling with color and trade. Not only will your stomachs be satisfied, you may be able to check out the numerous products on sale as well!

Quản Bạ Markets

There are multiple markets available in Quan Ba, all of which are worth a proper visit, whether it be to scavenge for food or to gaze at the products! If you don't have the time to go to all of them, select the one that will slot nicely with your time table.

Bún Chả Tân Sơn - Hà Giang

Well-respected with over 20 years of experience, Bun Cha Tay Son-Ha Giang restraunt is considered ideal and spacious too, agreeable if there are even large amounts of people popping over to get some delicious Bun Cha.
