SLIP INTO THE PAST

CANG BAC ME

Cang Bac Me is a prison built by the French that held Vietnamese prisoners that they would gradually kill. One of the darker and less-crowded sites, it is close to the Gam River, if you want to visit that too.

VUONG PALACE

An ancient palace (or rather, mansion) that belonged to the Hmong King, the Vuong Palace was built by the best Hmong builders and craftsmen from Yunnan, China.

DONG VAN ANCIENT TOWN

Some may shrug it off, and say that it is unimportant. Some will notice the remnants of French Colonisation and houses almost perfectly preserved from over a hundred years ago. Interested?
