ANY ONE IN NEED OF A GREAT VIEW?

LO RIVER

The Lo River is a large river that flows through part of Ha Giang and is surrounded by beautiful views. The people often plant crops by the banks as the water quality is good for the plants.

YEN MINH PINE FOREST

Sometimes compared to Dalat, this peaceful scene of undeniable beauty will imprint itself in your minds. Hmong people can be seen and the birds can be heard as you sit down and relax or take a short walk.

SUNG LA VALLEY

A beautiful landscape to feast on, Sung La Valley is a view into the lives of the people-- and the golden fields of flowers. If you fancy yourself a great landscape painter or drawer, bring along your set of pencils and paints!
